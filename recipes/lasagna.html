Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat sheets of pasta with various fillings and sauces, typically baked in the oven.The dish is baked until the top is golden and bubbly, and it's usually served in square slices. Lasagna is rich, hearty, and a favorite comfort food in many parts of the world.

Inredients

    For the Meat Sauce:
  • Ground beef: 1 lb (450g)
  • Italian sausage: 1 lb (450g) (optional for added flavor)
  • Onion: 1 (medium)
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes 1 can (28oz)
  • Tomato sauce: 1 can (15oz)
  • Tomato paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Basil: 2 teaspoons, dried
  • Oregano: 1 teaspoon, dried
  • Salt and pepper: To taste
  • Red pepper flakes: 1/4 teaspoon (optional for heat)
  • Fresh Parsley: 1/4 cup, chopped (optional for garnish)
      • For The Cheese Mixture:
  • Ricotta cheese: 1 container (15oz)
  • Egg: 1 (large)
  • Parmesan cheese: 1/4 cup, grated
  • Parsley: 1 tablespoon, fresh or dried
  • Salt and pepper: To taste
      • For The Lasagna Layers:
  • Lasagna noodles: 12 (regular or no-boil)
  • Mozzarella cheese: 4 cups, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese: 1/2 cup, grated

    • Steps

    1. Prepare The Meat Sauce:
      2.
      • Heat a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add ground beef, sausage (if using), and diced onion. Cook until the meat is browned and the onion is softened. Drain excess fat
      • Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant
      • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Simmer on low heat for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally
    2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
      3.
      • If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. (No-boil noodles can be used without pre-cooking)
    3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
      4.
      • In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth and well-combined
    4. Assemble the Lasagna:
      5.
      • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C)
      • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish
      • Add a layer of lasagna noodles (about 3 noodles per layer, slightly overlapping)
      • Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles
      • Top with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese
      • Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella) until you run out, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top
      • Sprinkle the remaining Parmesan cheese over the top
    5. Bake the Lasagna:
      6.
      • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent the cheese from browning too quickly)
      • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden brown
      • Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil if desired

    Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
